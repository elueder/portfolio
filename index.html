About Me

My name is Elizabeth Lueder, and I'm a web developer who enjoys full-stack projects and JavaScript logic.

I studied finance and economics at Boston College and recently completed General Assembly's Web Development Immersive Program. While I enjoyed the logic used in finance and the wide range of applications in economics, I craved a function in which I could use them more creatively.

In between BC and GA I worked at Staples on a team that set testing standards for development teams, which involved researching code quality tools, surveying directors of delivery teams, and compiling data. My favorite part of my job was finding ways to automate data entry and speed up processes, which led me to pursuing a track in web development.

I am looking for an opportunity where I can work on a wide breadth of full-stack development projects with a small team of determined developers. I look forward to working in an industry in which I can make an impact.
